How much does a concrete patio or driveway cost?+

It depends on size, access, tear-out, and finish. That's why estimates are free — we come out, measure, and give you a firm number before any work starts.

Do you handle tear-out of old concrete?+

Yes — demolition, haul-off, and debris removal are part of the job. We break out cracked slabs, re-compact the subgrade, and pour new.

How long before I can walk or drive on it?+

Foot traffic after 24–48 hours; vehicles after about 7 days. Concrete keeps gaining strength for weeks — we'll give you exact guidance for your pour and the weather.

Will my concrete crack?+

All concrete moves — the trick is controlling where. Proper subgrade compaction, rebar where the job calls for it, and control joints or saw cuts keep cracking inside the joints, not across your slab.

What area do you serve?+

We're based in New Haven, MO and serve the greater St. Louis area. Not sure if you're in range? Call — worst case we point you to someone good.
